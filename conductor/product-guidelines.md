# Product Guidelines — BGS Travel and Tourism

## Brand Identity
**BGS Travel and Tourism** — Dubai's home-grown, globally connected travel company.

## Voice & Tone
- **Warm** — Like a trusted friend who knows every destination personally
- **Expert** — Confident, specific, authoritative without being cold
- **Inspiring** — Every line should make the reader want to pack their bags
- **Grounded** — Not over-hyped luxury fluff. Real experiences, real detail.

### Tone examples
| Wrong | Right |
|---|---|
| "Amazing unforgettable experiences!" | "Dune bashing at sunset. BBQ under the stars. Home by midnight." |
| "We are a leading travel company" | "Our team in Arusha has been guiding safaris since 2014." |
| "Best prices guaranteed" | "Found it cheaper? We'll match it. No questions." |

## Design Principles

### 1. Dubai First
The homepage, packages, and hero content leads with Dubai. Every other destination is secondary.

### 2. Black & Gold — Elevated by Travel Colour
- Primary palette: **Black/Deep Navy + Gold**
- Supporting palette: destination-specific accent colours (desert teal, safari earth, ocean blue, tropical green)
- Never dilute the premium feel — colour accents are used sparingly to evoke destination

### 3. Mobile First
Over 70% of UAE users browse on mobile. WhatsApp CTAs, sticky bottom bar, and touch-friendly cards are non-negotiable.

### 4. Conversion Over Decoration
Every section has a job. Hero → captures interest. Packages → drives clicks. WhatsApp section → captures lead. Beautiful design that doesn't convert is wasted.

### 5. CMS-Managed Content
All package cards, destination pages, blog posts, hotel listings, and transport options must be manageable by non-technical staff via WordPress admin. No hardcoded content that requires a developer to update.

### 6. Trust First
Dubai Tourism licence, review counts, years in business, and WhatsApp availability must be visible on every key page. Trust is BGS's biggest differentiator.

## Typography
- **Display / Headings:** Cormorant (Google Fonts) — weights 400, 600, 700
- **Body / UI:** Montserrat (Google Fonts) — weights 300, 400, 500, 600, 700

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: 1280px
- Section vertical padding: 80px desktop / 48px mobile
- Card border radius: 8px
- Button border radius: 4px

## Imagery Guidelines
- Always prefer authentic, non-stock-looking photography
- Dubai: Aerial shots, golden hour, desert landscapes, iconic skyline
- Africa: Wildlife in natural light, authentic guide interactions, landscape vastness
- People: Diverse travellers — families, couples, solo — never posed awkwardly
- No watermarks, no obvious iStock/Getty watermarks
