# Product Definition — BGS Travel and Tourism

## Project Name
BGS Travel and Tourism

## Description
Dubai-first travel and tourism website offering bespoke packages, guided tours, day trips, and excursions across the UAE and 14 additional global destinations — with offices on the ground in every market we sell.

## Problem Statement
Travellers in and visiting Dubai need a trusted, locally-rooted travel company that handles everything end-to-end: from iconic Dubai desert safaris to complex multi-country African safaris, Asian journeys, and beyond. Most online travel companies are aggregators without local expertise. BGS has physical offices in every destination it sells — this website must communicate that trust and convert visitors into bookings.

## Target Users
- **Primary:** UAE residents (expats + locals) seeking weekend trips, holiday packages, and international travel
- **Secondary:** International tourists arriving in Dubai seeking curated day experiences and excursions
- **Tertiary:** Pakistani, Indian, Indonesian diaspora in UAE seeking homeland travel + regional packages
- **Business:** Corporate groups seeking incentive travel and team experiences

## Key Goals
1. Establish BGS as Dubai's #1 trusted travel brand online
2. Drive package enquiries and bookings via WhatsApp, forms, and direct calls
3. Serve as a content hub (blog) for Dubai travel SEO dominance
4. Enable the BGS team to manage all content without developer dependency
5. Support GHL CRM integration for lead capture, follow-up, and automation
