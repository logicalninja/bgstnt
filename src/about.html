OUR STORY

Born in Dubai.
Built for the World.

BGS Travel & Tourism started with one belief — travel should be effortless, personal, and unforgettable. Ten years later, we have offices in 15 countries and have helped over 10,000 travellers see the world.

Our Background

Who We Are

BGS Travel & Tourism LLC is headquartered in Dubai, UAE — and licensed by the Dubai Department of Economy and Tourism. We're not an aggregator, a comparison site, or a booking engine. We're a team of real travel specialists with real offices in every destination we sell.

From our main office in Bur Dubai, we coordinate trips to 15 destinations across Africa, Asia, Europe, and the Americas. In each location, we have our own staff — guides, drivers, accommodation partners — not third-party agents we hand you off to.

That means when you're on safari in Tanzania, there's a BGS team member on the ground. When your visa for Uganda needs chasing, we're calling Kampala directly. When your family lands in Bali, someone who works for us is waiting.
